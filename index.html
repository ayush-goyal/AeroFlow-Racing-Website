Who We Are

We are AeroFlow Racing, a team of five juniors (left to right: Ayush Goyal, Poojan Mehta, Arul Gupta, Arya Mevada, and Shivam Patel from the Wheeler Center for Advanced Studies in Marietta, GA. Our team has a certain drive to learn from our mistakes, improve our team unity, and progress to achieve the impossible. Through our experience at the 2017 United States National Competition, we have built a multitude of connections related to various aspects of the competition, developed a vast platform for communication and team branding, and understood the actions necessary to succeed in the this year’s national and world competitions. We are one team, with one goal, with one passion. We are AeroFlow Racing.

Our Team

Poojan Mehta

Design Engineer

Poojan plays a vital role in the design and production of the AeroFlow Racing F1 Car. He combines his knowledge of aerodynamics with his skills of computer aided design on Autodesk to create a car that can exceed expectations. As lead engineer, he also contributes to the testing and manufacturing of the car using a CNC mill. Outside of his role on the team, Poojan is a varsity cross country runner and track star.

Arul Gupta

Executive Manager & Marketing Director

Through leading AeroFlow Racing, Arul has a strong drive to ensure the team’s success, organization, and keen time management. Additionally, Arul is tasked with raising funds and building connections for the team’s financial stability. With this, he can expand on his team and business management skills immensely. Outside of AeroFlow Racing, Arul is a competitive tennis player, participating in tournaments and as captain of the high school team.

Shivam Patel

Resource & Outreach Director

Shivam’s role is to make sure the team has the necessary resources for various aspects of the competition such the pit display, as well as the manufacturing of the car. In addition, Shivam is in charge of setting up outreach events in which AeroFlow racing are able to raise awareness for both the team as well as F1 in schools. Outside of the team, Shivam is interested in the field of medicine.

Ayush Goyal

Graphic Designer & Manufacturing Engineer

Branding of AeroFlow Racing is an integral part of the marketing of our team. Through his role, Ayush creates a variety of flyers, posters, and banners along with creating the team website. He is also tasked with manufacturing the team’s car, through which Ayush utilizes a CNC mill and makes each car design ready for testing. His other interests include participating in math team and being an avid programmer.

The Challenge

A global STEM competition

  • 20,000,000 Students
  • 40 Countries
  • 1 Experience

F1 in Schools is a worldwide competition consisting of several teams made up of kids aged 11-18. In this vastly competitive league, teams of 3-6 develop cars utilizing CAD and other related tools. However, the complexity of what we do reaches beyond mere engineering and into fields of business/marketing, graphic design, and possibly most importantly if considering real-world application, team management and cooperation. Then, each of these skill sets developed throughout the team process is put to the test at regional, national, and world level competitions. As a result, F1 in Schools has created a system which effectively develops and enhances students’ learning experience through their interests as well as by teaching necessary real-world situations/applications.

Past Results

The AeroFlow Racing team just returned from a very successful 2017 F1 in Schools National Finals in Austin, Texas. The competition lasted 3 days where we had the opportunity to witness several other teams and students across the Nation, compete against those teams, give a verbal presentation to a table of judges, meet with colleges, and much more. The competition encompassed several different judging criteria and we were just one team out of fifteen total competing.

Although we did not qualify for the World Competition, we placed fifth overall, and received three various awards (Best Verbal Presentation, Best Marketing and Sponsorship Strategy, and Most Innovative Team) out of ten possible. Above all, we learned an immense amount about the improve to ensure our ability to make the World Competition in 2018.
